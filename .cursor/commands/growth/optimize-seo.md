# Optimize SEO

SEO optimization checklist covering on-page, technical, and content SEO.

## What This Command Does

Optimizes SEO by:
- Reviewing on-page SEO elements
- Checking technical SEO
- Analyzing content quality
- Identifying optimization opportunities
- Creating optimization plan

## How It Works

1. **Understand SEO Goals**
   - Ask about pages to optimize
   - Identify target keywords
   - Understand SEO goals
   - Get current SEO data

2. **Perform SEO Audit**
   - Review on-page elements
   - Check technical SEO
   - Analyze content
   - Identify issues

3. **Generate Optimization Plan**
   - Create SEO recommendations
   - Prioritize improvements
   - Suggest specific changes
   - Provide implementation steps

## What to Tell the User

- "What pages do you want to optimize for SEO?"
- "What are your target keywords?"
- Perform SEO audit and generate plan
- "SEO optimization complete! Issues: [list]. Recommendations: [list]. Priority: [high/medium/low]"
