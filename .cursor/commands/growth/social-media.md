# Social Media

Optimize social media presence including sharing, Open Graph, and social analytics.

## What This Command Does

Optimizes social media by:
- Optimizing social sharing
- Configuring Open Graph tags
- Setting up social analytics
- Adding social proof
- Optimizing social content
- Tracking social performance

## How It Works

1. **Understand Social Goals**
   - Ask about social objectives
   - Identify social platforms
   - Understand sharing needs
   - Get context on goals

2. **Optimize Social Sharing**
   - Configure share buttons
   - Optimize share content
   - Add share tracking
   - Test sharing functionality

3. **Configure Open Graph**
   - Set up Open Graph tags
   - Optimize social previews
   - Add Twitter Cards
   - Configure social images
   - Test social previews

4. **Set Up Social Analytics**
   - Configure social tracking
   - Set up share analytics
   - Track social engagement
   - Analyze social performance

5. **Add Social Proof**
   - Integrate social proof widgets
   - Add testimonials
   - Show social signals
   - Display social metrics
