# Content Marketing

Optimize content marketing strategy including blog posts, content calendar, and SEO optimization.

## What This Command Does

Manages content marketing by:
- Optimizing blog posts for SEO
- Creating content calendar
- SEO content optimization
- Content performance tracking
- Content strategy planning
- Content distribution planning

## How It Works

1. **Understand Content Goals**
   - Ask about content objectives
   - Identify target keywords
   - Understand audience
   - Get context on content

2. **Optimize Content**
   - SEO optimize blog posts
   - Optimize meta tags
   - Add structured data
   - Optimize images
   - Improve readability

3. **Create Content Calendar**
   - Plan content schedule
   - Identify content topics
   - Schedule publications
   - Plan content themes
   - Coordinate with campaigns

4. **Track Content Performance**
   - Set up content analytics
   - Track content metrics
   - Analyze performance
   - Identify top content
   - Generate insights

## What to Tell the User

- "What content do you want to optimize?"
- "What are your content goals?"
- Optimize content
- "Content optimization complete! SEO: [improved]. Calendar: [created]. Performance tracking: [setup]"
