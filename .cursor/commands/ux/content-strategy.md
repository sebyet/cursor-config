# Content Strategy

Plan content strategy and messaging to align content with user goals and business objectives.

## What This Command Does

Creates content strategy by:
- Planning content structure and hierarchy
- Defining content tone and voice
- Creating content guidelines
- Planning content for different user journeys
- Optimizing content for user goals

## How It Works

1. **Understand Content Needs**
   - Ask about content types and goals
   - Identify target audience and personas
   - Understand business objectives
   - Get context on existing content
   - Determine content scope

2. **Plan Content Strategy**
   - Define content structure and hierarchy
   - Establish content tone and voice
   - Create content guidelines and standards
   - Plan content for user journeys
   - Define content goals and metrics

3. **Create Content Guidelines**
   - Document tone and voice
   - Define content style and format
   - Create content templates
   - Establish content governance
   - Document content best practices

4. **Generate Content Strategy Document**
   - Create comprehensive strategy document
   - Include content structure and hierarchy
   - Document tone, voice, and guidelines
   - Plan content for journeys
   - Make it actionable for content creation

## What to Tell the User

- "What content needs to be strategized?"
- "Who is the target audience?"
- "What are the content goals?"
- Create content strategy
- "Content strategy created! Structure: [summary]. Tone: [defined]. Guidelines: [created]."
