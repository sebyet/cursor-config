# Create Research Plan

Plan user research studies including interviews, usability testing, and surveys.

## What This Command Does

Creates comprehensive research plans that include:
- Research objectives and questions
- Methodology (interviews, surveys, usability tests)
- Participant recruitment criteria
- Research script or guide
- Timeline and logistics
- Analysis approach

## How It Works

1. **Understand Research Goals**
   - Ask about what you want to learn
   - Identify research questions
   - Understand target users
   - Determine research type needed

2. **Design Research Plan**
   - Choose methodology
   - Define participant criteria
   - Create research script/guide
   - Plan timeline and logistics

3. **Generate Plan Document**
   - Structure research plan
   - Include all necessary sections
   - Add templates for scripts

## What to Tell the User

- "What do you want to learn from this research?"
- "What research questions do you have?"
- "Who are your target users?"
- Generate research plan
- "Research plan created! Ready to recruit participants."
