# Risk Assessment

Perform risk assessment and identify mitigation strategies.

## What This Command Does

Performs risk assessment by:
- Identifying risks and threats
- Assessing risk severity
- Evaluating risk probability
- Creating mitigation strategies
- Generating risk report

## How It Works

1. **Understand Risk Assessment Needs**
   - Ask about assessment scope
   - Identify systems to assess
   - Understand risk categories
   - Determine assessment depth

2. **Assess Risks**
   - Identify risks
   - Assess severity
   - Evaluate probability
   - Create mitigations

3. **Generate Report**
   - Create risk report
   - List risks
   - Provide mitigations
   - Document assessment

## What to Tell the User

- "What systems or areas should we assess?"
- "What risk categories are important?"
- Perform risk assessment and generate report
- "Risk assessment complete! Risks: [count]. High priority: [list]. Mitigations: [strategies]"
