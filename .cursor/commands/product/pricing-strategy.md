# Pricing Strategy

Design pricing models, optimize pricing, and analyze pricing experiments for revenue optimization.

## What This Command Does

Manages pricing by:
- Designing pricing models
- Optimizing pricing
- Running pricing experiments
- Analyzing revenue impact
- Comparing pricing strategies
- Generating pricing recommendations

## How It Works

1. **Understand Pricing Goals**
   - Ask about pricing objectives
   - Identify pricing constraints
   - Understand market context
   - Get context on goals

2. **Design Pricing Model**
   - Create pricing structure
   - Define pricing tiers
   - Set pricing levels
   - Design pricing strategy
   - Plan pricing experiments

3. **Optimize Pricing**
   - Analyze pricing data
   - Test pricing changes
   - Optimize pricing tiers
   - Adjust pricing strategy
   - Measure pricing impact

4. **Run Pricing Experiments**
   - Set up pricing tests
   - Configure experiment variants
   - Track pricing metrics
   - Analyze experiment results
   - Generate pricing insights

5. **Analyze Revenue Impact**
   - Calculate revenue impact
   - Analyze pricing elasticity
   - Track revenue metrics
   - Compare pricing strategies
   - Generate pricing report

## What to Tell the User

- "What pricing strategy do you want to develop?"
- "What are your pricing goals?"
- Design pricing strategy
- "Pricing strategy complete! Model: [details]. Experiments: [configured]. Revenue impact: [analysis]"
