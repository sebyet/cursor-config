# Analyze Feature Impact

Estimate business impact and metrics for features before and after implementation.

## What This Command Does

Analyzes potential feature impact by:
- Defining success metrics
- Estimating quantitative impact
- Identifying leading indicators
- Creating measurement plan
- Setting up tracking

## How It Works

1. **Understand the Feature**
   - Ask about the feature
   - Identify target users
   - Understand business goals

2. **Define Metrics**
   - Primary success metrics
   - Secondary metrics
   - Leading indicators
   - Baseline measurements

3. **Estimate Impact**
   - Projected impact on metrics
   - Confidence levels
   - Time to see results
   - Risk factors

4. **Create Measurement Plan**
   - How to track metrics
   - What data to collect
   - When to measure
   - Success thresholds

## What to Tell the User

- "What feature do you want to analyze?"
- "What are your primary business goals?"
- Define metrics and estimate impact
- Create measurement plan
- "Impact analysis complete! Track these metrics: [list]"
