# Go-to-Market

Plan go-to-market strategy including launch planning, market entry, and launch execution.

## What This Command Does

Plans GTM by:
- Creating GTM strategy
- Planning product launch
- Defining market entry strategy
- Creating launch checklist
- Coordinating launch activities
- Tracking launch metrics

## How It Works

1. **Understand GTM Goals**
   - Ask about product launch
   - Identify target market
   - Understand launch objectives
   - Get context on timeline

2. **Create GTM Strategy**
   - Define target audience
   - Create messaging strategy
   - Plan distribution channels
   - Design launch sequence
   - Set launch goals

3. **Plan Product Launch**
   - Create launch timeline
   - Plan launch activities
   - Coordinate launch team
   - Set up launch tracking
   - Create launch checklist

4. **Define Market Entry**
   - Analyze market opportunity
   - Plan market entry strategy
   - Identify key partnerships
   - Plan competitive positioning
   - Set market goals

5. **Create Launch Plan**
   - Finalize launch checklist
   - Assign launch tasks
   - Set launch milestones
   - Configure launch tracking
   - Generate GTM report

## What to Tell the User

- "What product are you launching?"
- "What's your target market?"
- Create GTM strategy
- "Go-to-market plan complete! Strategy: [details]. Launch checklist: [ready]. Timeline: [schedule]"
